Ideas

Name Context & Problem Solution
1 PhD Job Search Many students struggle to find effective PhD job postings or research teams that align with their interests. How to effectively find research teams that match students' interests? 1. Extract author lists from major journals and conferences, identify research teams based on these lists, and summarize the results using artificial intelligence. 2. Extract content from websites relevant to PhD job postings.
2 Smart grocery price compare Grocery prices vary widely across stores (Carrefour, Lidl, Monoprix, etc.). Users waste time checking multiple apps, and unit sizes (500 g vs 1 kg) make comparison difficult. An app that compares real-time grocery prices across nearby supermarkets. It converts all items to €/kg or €/L, calculates the total basket cost, and shows the cheapest store nearby.
3 SmartWardrobe-Outfit Planner & Tracker Many people struggle every morning to decide what to wear or forget which clothes they have. This wastes time and often leads to wearing the same outfits repeatedly while other clothes stay unused. A mobile app that lets users photograph and categorize their clothes, then uses simple AI to suggest daily outfit combinations based on weather, occasion, and previous usage. It also tracks worn frequency to help declutter the wardrobe and optimize choices and changing outfits frequently
4 EcoRemind – Waste Sorting Assistant Many people are confused about how to properly sort household waste, especially since recycling rules differ between cities (me first). Incorrect waste sorting causes contamination of recyclable materials and increases the environmental footprint. EcoRemind is a mobile app that allows users to scan a product’s barcode or take a photo to identify its material and find the correct recycling bin (plastic, glass, paper, etc.) according to their city’s rules. The app combines a visual recognition model and a geolocated database to provide accurate, eco-friendly guidance.
5 Alarm Randomizer I'm so used to listening to the same alarm sound that sometimes my brain would just filter out the sound in the morning and continue sleeping. Oversleeping can cause you to be late for school/work/events/... not a very common problem but some people do have this issue. If you have a different alarm sound each day, you will not be able to get used to it. Each alarm sound is engineered to be just loud and energetic enough to wake you without being harsh.
6 Balance diet helper International students and young adults in France who live alone and struggle to maintain balanced eating habits. Without family supervision, many eat irregularly or rely on repetitive, unbalanced meals (e.g., too much carbs, not enough vegetables or protein). This leads to low energy, poor concentration, and unhealthy weight changes. An app that analyzes daily meals, detects nutritional imbalance, and provides personalized meal suggestions based on missing nutrients. It helps users track calories, vitamins, and macronutrients, encouraging variety and moderation.
7 Find your phone wallpaper Changing your phone wallpaper is a complex task. Current wallpaper search methods rely on matching fixed phrases, failing to consider details like color and orientation. This makes it difficult to find the wallpaper you need. Introduce AGI (Artificial Intelligence Technology). Using AI's image recognition capabilities helps us find the wallpaper we want, making it more accurate and personalized.
8 Menu Assistant As a foreigner, when ordering food at a Parisian restaurant, I often can't figure out what the dishes look like on a full-text menu. To order something more satisfactory, I have to search, which takes a lot of time and is often inaccurate. Use an AI assistant to analyze the dish names to obtain the ingredients and crawl example images from the internet.
9 Dog breed picker It's hard to choose the perfect dog breed for you when you want a pet. An application that you can input info like if you want a big or small dog, how active it is, your budget, your house size, ... to recommend a suitable breed for you
10 Smart Fridge Organizer App Food in the fridge often expires or gets mixed (raw meat near vegetables or cooked food), causing odor and contamination. An app that tracks stored food, reminds users before expiration, and suggests optimal fridge placement to keep ingredients fresh and separated.
11 Website Change Detection In daily life, we might constantly check for updates on certain websites, but not every website (especially private websites) has an update notification interface. Users can register the websites they want to track. The backend will check the MD5 hash of the website content every half hour, and if changes are found, an update notification will be sent to the user's email. Furthermore, we are considering more granular update notification rules and introducing an AI assistant to summarize website update content.
12 Smart Wardrobe Finder App People with many clothes who often forget where specific items are stored. Closets are messy, and it’s hard to find clothes — users waste time searching or forget what they own. An app that lets users digitally organize their wardrobe by taking photos and tagging storage locations (drawer, box, section). It helps quickly locate any item through search, filters, or color recognition.
13 MedMinder – Smart Medicine Reminder & Tracker Many people (especially students or elderly) forget to take their medicine on time or lose track of their prescriptions. Missed doses reduce treatment effectiveness and can even cause health complications. MedMinder is a simple mobile app that reminds users when to take their medication, tracks intake history, and alerts them when they’re running low on doses. It can also generate a refill reminder and optionally share compliance reports with a doctor or caregiver :)
14 Take home assignments sharing platform people looking for jobs or just people that want to train their skills and prepare for future career Problems: There are many take home assignment formats, vary from company to company, on many different topics,... Young students might have problems when facing one if they have never done anything similar before. A website where people can share which type of assignments they got and how they worked on it. Of course only share if the company allows that.
15 Paris French Cuisine Michelin Planner App Paris has many Michelin restaurants with wide differences in price, location, and style. Users struggle to plan efficient routes and balanced budgets while enjoying diverse culinary experiences. The app records each dining experience and personal taste preference, then uses map-based and budget-planning algorithms to generate the optimal dining route. It ensures monthly or quarterly spending balance while recommending restaurants across different styles — traditional, modern, creative, or fusion French cuisine.
16 WashTrack Smart Laundry Queue & Reminder System In student residences (like where i live) or shared buildings, laundry rooms are often crowded, and users forget their clothes in the machine or arrive while all machines are occupied especially on week ends. There’s no simple way to know which machines are available, when a cycle will end, or to organize a fair waiting queue. WashTrack is a mobile/web app where users scan a QR code placed on each washing machine to register their laundry session. The app records the duration, notifies the user when the cycle is done, and updates the machine’s status in real time. Others can check availability and join a waiting list to be notified when a machine becomes free.
16 AI startup ideas generator the hardest part when creating a startup is to come up with an idea that you can turn into a product An AI startup company that uses AI to generate startup ideas for you.
17 AI-powered paper finder Input an academic statement, and it automatically uses AI to find whether each sentence has been used in other documents, thus reducing repetition of ideas. It can also be used as an assistant to find cited documents.
18 Cooking timer When cooking several dishes, it’s easy to lose track of what to do next — something burns, or ingredients are added too late. Help users cook efficiently, avoid mistakes, and enjoy stress-free multitasking in the kitchen. An app that manages multiple cooking timers and step-by-step reminders, telling you exactly when to flip, stir, or serve each dish.
19 EventMap – Discover Local Free Events Nearby Many people, especially students, don’t know what free or low-cost activities are happening around them (concerts, exhibitions, sports, meetups). Local events are often scattered across different websites or social media pages, making discovery complicated. EventMap is a website that aggregates free and affordable local events from public sources (city websites, associations, universities, cultural centers) and displays them on an interactive map. Users can filter by category (music, art, sports, networking) and save events to their calendar.
20 Nurse search platform When you have a surgery in France, you need to look for a nurse that will go to your house each day to replace the bandage and stuffs. Most nurse agencies have their own websites so a lot of navigating around. Doctorlib requires taking appointment beforehand. A centrailized platform that connects nurses and patients. Also allow rating and commenting.
