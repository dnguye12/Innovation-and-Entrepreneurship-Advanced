FoodPlan Maker

Presentation

FoodPlan Maker is a smart nutrition app that turns your favorite foods into balanced meal plans. It adapts to weight-loss, muscle-gain, or maintenance goals using your age, weight, height, and activity. The app only suggests foods you actually like or can eat, respecting allergies and intolerance. It calculates calories, protein, fats, carbs, vitamins, and minerals from your real meals.

Mission

  • FoodPlan Maker’s mission is to make healthy eating realistic, enjoyable, and tailored to each person’s tastes and lifestyle.
  • The app lets users choose the foods they’re willing to eat, then calculates portions that meet calorie, protein, and micronutrient needs.
  • It intelligently fills nutrient gaps using only acceptable foods and displays intuitive charts of calorie, macro, and vitamin/mineral coverage.
