FoodPlan Maker

Personalized Nutritional Needs & Alternatives.

Target Users

  • People trying to lose weight who need lower calories while staying nutritionally balanced.
  • People building muscle or gaining weight who need higher protein and stable caloric intake.
  • Individuals living alone or international students whose meals are often simple, repetitive, and nutritionally unbalanced.
  • People with strong food preferences, dislikes, intolerances, or allergies (e.g., no milk, no celery, no spicy food).

Problems

  1. Nutritional imbalance is common.

    Users often rely on one or two foods (rice, pasta, eggs, sandwiches), causing protein, vitamin, and mineral deficiencies.

  2. General nutrition apps recommend foods users do not like.

    Traditional diet tools may suggest foods the user hates (e.g., celery, milk, organ meats), making the plan impossible to follow.

  3. People living alone often eat irregularly or repetitively.

    Especially international students who cook simple meals but unintentionally miss essential nutrients.

  4. Weight-loss and muscle-building goals do not match actual meals.

    Users do not know how much protein, calories, or micronutrient their meals truly contain or how much they should eat.

Solution

By combining user food preferences, personal fitness goals, and adult nutritional standards, FoodPlan Maker provides a personalized, enjoyable, and nutritionally complete meal plan.

1. User selects foods they want to eat

Simply choose foods you are willing to eat today. No unwanted foods. No forced items.

2. App calculates recommended intake amounts

Using personal data (age, weight, height, goal), the app calculates daily needs for:

  • Total calories
  • Protein, fat, and carbohydrates
  • Key vitamins and minerals (Vitamin C, D, calcium, iron, zinc, fiber, etc.)

Then, based only on foods you like, it recommends how much of each food to eat (grams/servings) to approach the nutritional target.

3. Intelligent nutrient-gap completion

If the chosen foods lack certain nutrients, the app recommends additional foods only from items you can accept. No forcing disliked ingredients.

4. Nutrition score & visualization

The app shows how well you meet daily recommended intake through clear, visual feedback:

  • Calorie progress
  • Protein/fat/carbs distribution
  • Vitamin/mineral completeness
  • Weekly nutrition balance
  • Long-term progress toward body goals

FoodPlan Maker helps users eat healthier without forcing them to eat foods they dislike, by generating a personalized, enjoyable, and nutritionally complete meal plan.
